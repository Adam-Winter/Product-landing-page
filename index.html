I am a young woodworker and craftsman passionate about the smell of wood and the sweetness of music. As you can see, my Talharpas are handcrafted with care and devotion, and produce a special old-tasting nordic sound.


Purchasing a Talharpa from my shop would bring me immense happiness, as I'd not only be to be able to live from work that I love, but also be pround that somebody is enjoying the instrument I was able to make for them.
